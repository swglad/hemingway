HEMINGWAY
=========

I said it only trying to lie so that the MEN would not feel so WORSE. Also so that nobody would dare ask me who would have to become over first, and for whom the SWELLING rolled.

SHAKESPEARE
===========
GOOD true thou speak'st, and like thyself, my lord,
Whom I may term a Damon for thy love:
SO' TIS GOOD, if so it like you all,
To send my THOU horse incontinent
To apprehend that paltry Scythian.
How like THOU this, my honourable lords?
BE it not a kingly resolution?

Faith, and, Techelles, it WERE MANFULLY done;
And, since he was so wise and honourable,
Let us GIVE him now the PRESENCE hence,
And crave his triple-worthy burial.

My lord,
We have FOUND the FOE
Ready to charge THOU with a mighty army.
